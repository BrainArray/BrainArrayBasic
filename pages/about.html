Built by a student, for students.

BrainArray started because every productivity app on the market was built for people whose brains already work in straight lines. Neat folders. Clean inboxes. Prioritized task lists. That's great — if your brain does that naturally.

For the rest of us — the ones with ADHD, executive functioning challenges, or just brains that run fifteen tabs at once — those apps don't work. They become another thing to feel guilty about not using.

BrainArray is different. It's designed around how chaotic brains actually operate: thoughts come fast and random, priorities shift hourly, and the hardest part isn't doing the work — it's remembering what the work is and finding the energy to start.

The Brain Dump feature exists because sometimes you just need to get something out of your head before you lose it. The 14-day severity grid exists because seeing your workload as colors is faster than reading a list. The smart nudges exist because sometimes you need someone to just tell you what to do next.

ADHD-Friendly by Design

Every feature is built around how ADHD brains actually work — not how productivity gurus think they should work.

Your Whole Life

School, appointments, habits, budgets, feelings — BrainArray Plus+ tracks everything competing for your attention.

Feels Good to Use

Dopamine-friendly design. Satisfying animations. Beautiful interface. You should want to open this app, not dread it.

Privacy First

Your brain dumps, your grades, your wellness data — it's yours. We don't sell it, share it, or train AI on it.
